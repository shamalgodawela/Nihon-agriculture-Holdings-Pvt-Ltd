ABOUT US



NIHON AGRICULTURE HOLDINGS (PVT) LTD is a trusted name among Sri Lankan farmer for quality wide range of agri inputs including new generation Crop nutritional products, Crop protection product, Exotic and local vegetable Seeds, Agriculture Machineries, Agriculture Equipment, Micro Irrigation Systems, Installation of green houses, Plantation management services and consultation.

NIHON is dedicated to provide the best quality agri inputs from world renowned sources at an affordable price with over 15 years of experience of relevant fields.
NIHON AGRICULTURE HOLDINGS (PVT) LTD also trusted name globally for organic spices.
Ceylon spices are respected and highly valued in the whole world. In addition to taste, Ceylon spices are important key in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
We are one of the most reliable and consistent Organic Spices Exporters in Sri Lanka. Our spices are well-known in the global market for their variety, aroma, flavor.
